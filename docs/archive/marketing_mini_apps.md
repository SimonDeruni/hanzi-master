# 📱 The Marketing Funnel (Mini-Apps)

> **Strategic Concept:** These are lightweight, single-purpose apps designed to go viral on TikTok/Reels. They are offered for free to generate massive download numbers. Their primary business purpose is to act as a "Trojan Horse," funneling users to download the premium core app: **Hanzi Master**.

---

## 1. 🐉 The "Tattoo Roaster" (Cultural OCR)
*   **The Concept:** A specialized Magic Lens camera app. The user scans a real-world Chinese tattoo. The app translates it and uses local AI rules to politely "roast" it if it's gibberish (e.g., translating to "Chicken Noodle Soup" instead of "Strength").
*   **Viral Value:** People love seeing botched tattoos. A 10-second video of an app exposing a bad tattoo is guaranteed to hit the algorithm.
*   **The Funnel:** After roasting the tattoo, the app says, *"Don't make this mistake. Learn how to actually write Chinese with Hanzi Master."* -> Link to App Store.

## 2. 🌧️ "Radical Rain" (Survival Mini-Game)
*   **The Concept:** A fast-paced, Tetris-style arcade game. Radicals (氵, 木, 艹) fall from the top of the screen. The user must frantically swipe them together to fuse them into valid characters before they hit the bottom and fill the screen.
*   **Viral Value:** High-stress, visually chaotic gameplay that translates perfectly to short-attention-span platforms like TikTok gaming streams.
*   **The Funnel:** Every 3 "Game Overs," a beautiful, calming ad for Hanzi Master plays, contrasting the chaotic game with the zen experience of the main app.

## 3. ⚔️ "Calligraphy Duel" (Local Multiplayer)
*   **The Concept:** An iPad-optimized split-screen game. Two players sit across from each other. A Pinyin/English prompt appears. The first to draw the correct character with >80% accuracy wins the round.
*   **Viral Value:** Social, frantic, and highly visual. Perfect for short-form video showing friends screaming and scribbling at a coffee shop.
*   **The Funnel:** The game only includes 50 basic words. To unlock the full dictionary for dueling, they must download Hanzi Master.

## 4. 🖌️ "Water Ink" ASMR Simulator (Aesthetic Sandbox)
*   **The Concept:** A drawing mode focused purely on physics and sound. There is no grading. The ink visually bleeds, spreads, and fades based on finger velocity and pressure. Paired with high-quality ASMR brush-on-paper sound effects.
*   **Viral Value:** "Satisfying" digital art videos are a massive genre on social media. 
*   **The Funnel:** Users can only draw in black ink. To unlock the "Golden Brush" or "Midnight Blue," they must subscribe to the Hanzi Master ecosystem.
